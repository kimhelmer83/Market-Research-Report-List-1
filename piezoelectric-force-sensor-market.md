<p><ul><li>Diaphragm structure</li><li>Pistona structure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The piezoelectric force sensor market consists primarily of two types: diaphragm structure and piston structure sensors. Diaphragm structure sensors utilize a flexible diaphragm that bends under applied force, generating an electrical charge proportional to the force. In contrast, piston structure sensors feature a fixed cylinder with a movable piston that translates force into measurable pressure changes on a piezoelectric material. Both types cater to various applications, offering precise measurements and durability, thus driving demand across industries like automotive, aerospace, and industrial automation.</p></p>
