<p><p>The Nano Titanium Dioxide Powder market is primarily segmented into two types: Rutile and Anatase. Rutile-type titanium dioxide is known for its superior stability, higher refractive index, and excellent UV blocking properties, making it ideal for applications in coatings and plastics. In contrast, Anatase-type titanium dioxide is prized for its photocatalytic properties, making it suitable for environmental applications, such as water purification and air treatment. Both types serve distinct industrial needs, driving varied demand in the market.</p></p>
