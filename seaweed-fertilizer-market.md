<p><p>The seaweed fertilizer market primarily comprises two types: liquid seaweed fertilizer and powder seaweed fertilizer. Liquid seaweed fertilizer is a concentrated formulation, typically made from extracted seaweed, that can be easily mixed with water for application. It is valued for rapid nutrient absorption and convenience in use. On the other hand, powder seaweed fertilizer is made from dried, ground seaweed, offering a slow-release nutrient option. Both types enhance soil health, promote plant growth, and improve crop yield through natural bioactive compounds.</p></p>
