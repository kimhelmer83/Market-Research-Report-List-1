<p><p>The bio jet fuel market encompasses various types, notably Hydrogenated Vegetable Oil (HVO) and Fischer Tropsch (FT) fuels. HVO is produced by hydrogenating plant oils and fats, resulting in a renewable, drop-in fuel that enhances engine performance and reduces emissions. Fischer Tropsch technology transforms biomass or natural gas into synthetic jet fuel, offering high purity and stability. Both types are essential in advancing sustainable aviation fuel solutions, helping the aviation industry meet its environmental targets and reduce dependency on fossil fuels.</p></p>
