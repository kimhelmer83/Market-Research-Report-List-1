<p><p>The Gallium Arsenide High Electron Mobility Transistor (GaAs HEMT) market is categorized into two main types: enhanced and depletion-mode transistors. Enhanced-mode HEMTs are typically off when no voltage is applied, offering lower power consumption and increased efficiency, making them suitable for digital applications. In contrast, depletion-mode HEMTs are normally on and require a negative gate voltage to turn off, providing high power amplification and better performance in RF applications. Together, they cater to diverse electronic needs in telecommunications and other industries.</p></p>
