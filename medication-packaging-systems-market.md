<p><p>The medication packaging systems market includes several key types. Unit-dose packaging systems offer individual, pre-measured doses ideal for hospitals and long-term care facilities, enhancing safety and compliance. Multi-dose packaging systems contain multiple doses in a single package, often used in outpatient settings, simplifying administration for chronic therapies. Combi-dose packaging systems combine the benefits of both unit and multi-dose formats, allowing flexibility for patients who require varying doses, ultimately improving treatment adherence and efficiency in medication delivery.</p></p>
