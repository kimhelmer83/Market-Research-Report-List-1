<p><p>The latex tourniquet market is categorized into two main types: manual and pneumatic. Manual latex tourniquets are handheld devices that require a healthcare professional to apply and adjust tension, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Conversely, pneumatic latex tourniquets use automated systems to inflate and regulate pressure, providing precision and enhanced comfort for patients. Each type caters to different clinical needs, with manual variants typically utilized in everyday procedures, while pneumatic options are often preferred for complex surgical environments.</p></p>
