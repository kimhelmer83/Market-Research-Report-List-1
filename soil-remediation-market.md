<p><p>The soil remediation market encompasses various techniques to restore contaminated land. Bioremediation utilizes microorganisms to degrade pollutants naturally. Chemical oxidation involves applying oxidizing agents to break down contaminants. Stabilization/Solidification encapsulates harmful substances to prevent migration. Soil washing employs washing agents to extract pollutants from the soil. Thermal desorption uses heat to vaporize contaminants, allowing for their removal. Each method addresses specific types of pollutants and site conditions, contributing to effective environmental management and land revitalization.</p></p>
