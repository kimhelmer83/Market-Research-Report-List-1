<p><p>Whole-house ventilation solutions ensure optimal indoor air quality by addressing air exchange in residential settings. Exhaust ventilation systems remove stale air, creating a negative pressure that draws fresh air in. Supply ventilation systems, conversely, introduce filtered outdoor air, promoting a positive pressure indoors. Balanced ventilation systems combine both methods, ensuring equal rates of air exchange. Energy recovery systems enhance efficiency by transferring heat from outgoing air to incoming air, maintaining comfortable indoor temperatures while conserving energy.</p></p>
