<p><ul><li>Cell Analyzers</li><li>Cell Sorters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flow cytometry instrument market is primarily categorized into cell analyzers and cell sorters. Cell analyzers are designed to rapidly measure and analyze physical and chemical properties of cells in a fluid stream, providing valuable data on cell size, structure, and granularity. In contrast, cell sorters are equipped with advanced technology to isolate specific cell populations based on predefined criteria, enabling further studies or applications. Both types are essential in research, clinical diagnostics, and therapeutic development.</p></p>
