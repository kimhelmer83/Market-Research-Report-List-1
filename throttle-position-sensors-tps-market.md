<p><p>The Throttle Position Sensor (TPS) market comprises primarily two types: End Switches Type and Potentiometer Type. End Switches Type sensors detect the fully open or closed position of the throttle, offering a binary output ideal for specific applications. In contrast, Potentiometer Type sensors provide a variable voltage output corresponding to the throttle's angle, allowing for precise control and feedback in engine management systems. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing engine performance, emissions control, and enhancing overall vehicle efficiency.</p></p>
